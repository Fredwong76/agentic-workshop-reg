Workshop Series Plan: Agentic IDEs as a New Way of Working (NWOW)
Objective
Equip teams across the company to understand, experiment with, and adopt Agentic IDEs as a new, efficient way of working—reducing friction in AI development, accelerating solution delivery, and embedding responsible AI practices.
Series Overview
1. Executive & Team Awareness
2. Foundations & Capabilities
3. Hands-on Basics
4. Applied Use Cases
5. Responsible AI & Governance
6. Adoption & Roadmap
Detailed Workshop Plan
Session 1 — Introduction & Management Alignment
- Create awareness of Agentic IDEs
- Align leaders on value, benefits, urgency
- Output: Management buy-in, priority areas
Session 2 — Capabilities Deep Dive
- Demonstrate end-to-end capabilities
- Multi-language toolchain integration
- Output: Understanding of capabilities, initial use cases
Session 3 — Hands-On Fundamentals
- Build basic competence
- Exercises: code generation, data pipeline, microservice deployment
- Output: Practitioners gain hands-on experience
Session 4 — Business Use Case Labs
- Teams prototype real use cases
- Output: Working prototypes, prioritized backlog
Session 5 — Responsible AI & Governance
- Built-in guardrails, compliance, auditability
- Output: Alignment with Risk/Compliance/Security
Session 6 — Adoption Strategy & NWOW Rollout
- Maturity model, talent uplift, roadmap
- Output: Draft rollout plan, defined ownership
